## Weak Two Bids

*(Page 8)*

2♦/2♥/2♠ show **5-11 HCP** with a **6-card suit**. This is a key feature of SAYC.

### Requirements

- 6-card suit of reasonable quality (typically 2+ honors)
- No void
- No outside 4-card major
- Not suitable for 1-level opening

### Responses to Weak 2♦/2♥/2♠

| Response | HCP | Meaning |
|----------|-----|---------|
| PASS | 0-13 | No game interest |
| 2NT(!) | 15+ | **Feature ask**, forcing |
| Raise to 3 | — | Invitational or preemptive |
| Raise to game | — | To play (often preemptive) |
| New suit | 15+ | Forcing one round (RONF) |
| 3NT | 15-17 | To play, stoppers |

### After 2NT Feature Ask

**Opener's Rebids:**

| Rebid | HCP | Meaning |
|-------|-----|---------|
| 3 of own suit | 5-8 | Minimum, no outside feature |
| New suit | 8-11 | **Feature** (outside A or protected K) |
| 3NT | 9-11 | Maximum with solid suit (AKQxxx) |

### Reverse Bids

A **reverse** is a new suit at the 2-level that is **higher-ranking than the opening suit**. Shows 17+ HCP, typically 5-4 or better shape.

**Examples:**
- 1♣ - 1♠ - 2♦ = Reverse (diamonds higher than clubs)
- 1♣ - 1♠ - 2♥ = Reverse (hearts higher than clubs)
- 1♦ - 1♠ - 2♥ = Reverse (hearts higher than diamonds)

## Ace Asking (4NT)

*(Page 6)*

4NT is **Blackwood** asking for aces (after suit agreement).

### Responses to 4NT

| Response | Aces |
|----------|------|
| 5♣ | **0 or 4** aces |
| 5♦ | 1 ace |
| 5♥ | 2 aces |
| 5♠ | 3 aces |

### King Asking (5NT)

5NT after 4NT asks for kings (guarantees all 4 aces, invites grand slam).

| Response | Kings |
|----------|-------|
| 6♣ | 0 or 4 kings |
| 6♦ | 1 king |
| 6♥ | 2 kings |
| 6♠ | 3 kings |

### Interference Over Blackwood (DOPI)

| Action | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Double | 0 aces |
| Pass | 1 ace |

## About This System

This is the **Standard American Yellow Card (SAYC)**, the official ACBL bidding system created in the 1980s as a standardized method for tournament play and casual partnerships. It is the default system on BBO (Bridge Base Online).

**Core framework:**
- 5-card majors
- 15-17 NT
- Strong artificial 2♣ (22+)
- **Weak two bids** (2♦/2♥/2♠)
- Stayman and Jacoby Transfers
- **Jacoby 2NT** (forcing major raise)
- Blackwood 4NT (0 or 4 / 1 / 2 / 3)
- Gerber 4♣ (in NT auctions)
- Negative doubles
- Michaels and Unusual 2NT

**Key differences from [our partnership system](SYSTEM.md):**
- Opening range: **13-21** (not 12-19)
- 2♦/2♥/2♠: **Weak** 5-11, 6 cards (not strong 20-22, 5+ cards)
- 2♣: **22+** (not 17+ with game in hand)
- Blackwood 5♣: **0 or 4 aces** (not 0 only)
- 2-level overcall: **10-16** (not 8-16)
- Major raise: **Jacoby 2NT** (not Weak Suit Asking)

See [SAYC-DIFFERENCES.md](SAYC-DIFFERENCES.md) for a detailed comparison.
