## System Identification

Our system is a **Standard Natural bidding system** with these characteristics:

| Feature | Our System | SAYC Standard |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| 1NT opening | 15-17 HCP | 15-17 HCP |
| 2♣ opening | 22+ HCP, strong artificial | 22+ HCP |
| 2NT opening | 20-21 HCP | 20-21 HCP |
| Major openings | 5+ cards required | 5+ cards |
| Weak twos | 5-10 HCP, 6-card suit | 5-11 HCP, 6-card |
| Stayman | 2♣ over 1NT | Yes |
| Transfers | Jacoby (2♦/2♥ over 1NT) | Yes |
| Ace asking | Blackwood 4NT | Yes |

## Key Conventions Reference

### Stayman Convention
- **Purpose:** Find 4-4 major fit after 1NT opening
- **Bid:** 2♣ over 1NT
- **Responses:** 2♦ (no major), 2♥ (4+ hearts), 2♠ (4+ spades)

### Jacoby Transfer Bids
- **Purpose:** Transfer to major suit, keep strong hand hidden
- **Bids:** 2♦ = transfer to ♥, 2♥ = transfer to ♠
- **Note:** Opener must accept the transfer

### Blackwood 4NT
- **Purpose:** Ask for aces before slam
- **Responses:** 5♣ (0), 5♦ (1), 5♥ (2), 5♠ (3)

### Splinter Bids
- **Purpose:** Show support + shortness for slam investigation
- **Bids:** Double jump in new suit (e.g., 1♥ - 4♣)
- **Meaning:** 4+ trump support, singleton/void in bid suit
